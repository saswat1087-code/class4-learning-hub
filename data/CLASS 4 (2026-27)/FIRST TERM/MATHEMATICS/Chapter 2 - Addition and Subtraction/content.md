# Addition and Subtraction (5-Digit Numbers)

## Content
**Addition with Regrouping (Carrying):**

When adding 5-digit numbers, we add from right to left (ones place first). If the sum in any column is 10 or more, we carry over to the next column.

Example: 45,678 + 32,456 = ?

Steps:
1. Add ones: 8 + 6 = 14 (write 4, carry 1)
2. Add tens: 7 + 5 + 1 = 13 (write 3, carry 1)
3. Add hundreds: 6 + 4 + 1 = 11 (write 1, carry 1)
4. Add thousands: 5 + 2 + 1 = 8 (write 8)
5. Add ten thousands: 4 + 3 = 7

Answer: 78,134

**Subtraction with Borrowing (Regrouping):**

When subtracting, if a digit is smaller than the digit below it, we borrow from the left column.

Example: 78,543 - 23,789 = ?

Steps:
1. Ones: 3 - 9 (cannot), borrow from tens → 13 - 9 = 4
2. Tens: 3 - 8 (cannot), borrow from hundreds → 13 - 8 = 5
3. Hundreds: 4 - 7 (cannot), borrow from thousands → 14 - 7 = 7
4. Thousands: 7 - 3 = 4
5. Ten thousands: 7 - 2 = 5

Answer: 54,754

**Word Problem Strategy:**
1. Read carefully - what is being asked?
2. Identify key numbers
3. Decide: add or subtract?
4. Solve step by step
5. Check your answer

## Key Points
- Add numbers from right to left (ones place first)
- Carry over when sum is 10 or more
- Borrow from left when top digit is smaller
- Use inverse operations to check answers
- Always line up numbers by their place values

## Vocabulary
- **Addition**: Combining numbers to find the total (sum)
- **Subtraction**: Finding the difference between numbers
- **Regrouping**: Rearranging numbers (carrying/borrowing)
- **Carry Over**: Moving extra value to the next column
- **Borrow**: Taking value from the left column
- **Inverse Operation**: Opposite operation (addition and subtraction are inverses)
- **Sum**: The answer in addition
- **Difference**: The answer in subtraction

## Fun Fact
