# Division

## Content
Division is sharing equally. It is the opposite of multiplication. For example, 20 ÷ 4 means sharing 20 items among 4 groups = 5 items each.

**Parts of Division:**
- **Dividend**: The number being divided (inside)
- **Divisor**: The number you are dividing by (outside)
- **Quotient**: The answer
- **Remainder**: What is left over (if any)

**Checking Division:** Multiply quotient × divisor and add remainder.
1135 × 5 = 5675 + 3 = 5678 ✓

**Dividing by 10, 100, 1000:**
- Divide by 10: Move decimal one place left (5678 ÷ 10 = 567.8)
- Divide by 100: Move decimal two places left (5678 ÷ 100 = 56.78)
- Divide by 1000: Move decimal three places left (5678 ÷ 1000 = 5.678)

## Key Points
- Division means sharing equally
- Dividend ÷ Divisor = Quotient (Remainder)
- Check your answer: (Quotient × Divisor) + Remainder = Dividend
- Remainder is always smaller than the divisor
- Use long division for larger numbers

## Vocabulary
- **Division**: Sharing equally
- **Dividend**: Number being divided
- **Divisor**: Number you divide by
- **Quotient**: The answer
- **Remainder**: What is left over
- **Long Division**: Step-by-step division method

## Fun Fact
The division sign (÷) is called an "obelus". It was first used in 1659 by Swiss mathematician Johann Rahn! ➗
