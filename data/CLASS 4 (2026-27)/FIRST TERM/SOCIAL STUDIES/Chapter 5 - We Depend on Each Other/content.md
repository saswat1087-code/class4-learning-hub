# We Depend on Each Other

## Content
No one can live alone. We all depend on each other for our needs. This is called interdependence.

**Types of Dependence:**

1. **Within Family:**
   - Parents care for children
   - Children help parents with small tasks
   - Grandparents share wisdom and stories

2. **Within Community:**
   - Farmers grow food
   - Shopkeepers sell food
   - Doctors treat illnesses
   - Teachers educate children
   - Police keep us safe
   - Firefighters help in emergencies

3. **Between States and Countries:**
   - One state may grow rice, another grows wheat
   - Countries trade goods with each other
   - We import oil, export spices
   - We learn from each other's technology

**How We Depend on Each Other:**
- **Food**: Farmers, transporters, shopkeepers
- **Shelter**: Builders, electricians, plumbers
- **Clothes**: Cotton growers, weavers, tailors
- **Education**: Teachers, book publishers, school builders
- **Health**: Doctors, nurses, medicine makers
- **Safety**: Police, soldiers, firefighters

**Cooperation:**
Working together helps us achieve more. Teamwork makes tasks easier and faster.

## Key Points
- Everyone depends on others for their needs
- Different people do different jobs
- Communities work together to help each other
- Countries trade goods with other countries
- Cooperation makes life better for everyone

## Vocabulary
- **Interdependence**: Depending on each other
- **Community**: Group of people living in an area
- **Trade**: Buying and selling goods
- **Import**: Bringing goods from another country
- **Export**: Sending goods to another country
- **Cooperation**: Working together
- **Service**: Work done to help others

## Fun Fact
One T-shirt travels through 5-6 countries before you buy it! Cotton may come from India, fabric made in China, sewn in Bangladesh, and sold in USA! 👕
