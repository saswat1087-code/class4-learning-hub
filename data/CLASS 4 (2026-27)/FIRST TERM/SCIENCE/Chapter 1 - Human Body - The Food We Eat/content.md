# Human Body: The Food We Eat

## Content
Food gives us energy to grow, work, and stay healthy. Different foods provide different nutrients.

**Types of Nutrients:**
- **Carbohydrates**: Give energy (rice, bread, potatoes)
- **Proteins**: Help growth and repair (eggs, milk, pulses, meat)
- **Fats**: Give energy and keep us warm (butter, oil, nuts)
- **Vitamins**: Protect from diseases (fruits, vegetables)
- **Minerals**: Strong bones and teeth (calcium from milk, iron from greens)

**Balanced Diet:**
A balanced diet contains all nutrients in the right amounts.

**Digestive System:**
1. **Mouth**: Teeth chew food, saliva starts digestion
2. **Food pipe (Oesophagus)**: Carries food to stomach
3. **Stomach**: Mixes food with digestive juices
4. **Small Intestine**: Absorbs nutrients into blood
5. **Large Intestine**: Absorbs water
6. **Anus**: Removes waste

**Healthy Eating Habits:**
- Eat meals at regular times
- Drink plenty of water
- Eat fruits and vegetables daily
- Avoid too much sugar and junk food
- Chew food properly

## Key Points
- Different foods give different nutrients
- A balanced diet keeps us healthy
- The digestive system breaks down food
- Nutrients are absorbed in the small intestine
- Water is essential for our body

## Vocabulary
- **Nutrients**: Substances in food that help our body
- **Digestion**: Breaking down food into simpler form
- **Balanced Diet**: Food with all nutrients
- **Carbohydrates**: Energy-giving foods
- **Proteins**: Body-building foods
- **Vitamins**: Protective foods

## Fun Fact
The small intestine is about 6-7 meters long (20-22 feet) - longer than a car! It is called "small" because of its width, not length! 🧬
