# Push and Pull (Force)

## Content
A force is a push or a pull that can make things move, stop, or change direction.

**Pushes:**
- Kicking a ball
- Pushing a door to open it
- Pushing a trolley

**Pulls:**
- Opening a drawer
- Pulling a rope
- Pulling a suitcase

**Effects of Force:**
- Make a stationary object move
- Stop a moving object
- Change the speed of an object
- Change the direction of an object
- Change the shape of an object

**Types of Forces:**
- **Gravitational Force**: Pulls everything toward Earth
- **Magnetic Force**: Push or pull from magnets
- **Frictional Force**: Opposes motion
- **Muscular Force**: Force from our muscles

## Key Points
- Force is a push or a pull
- We use force every day in many activities
- Force can start, stop, or change motion
- Gravity pulls everything down
- Friction slows things down

## Vocabulary
- **Force**: A push or a pull
- **Gravity**: Force that pulls things toward Earth
- **Friction**: Force that opposes motion
- **Motion**: Movement of an object
- **Speed**: How fast something moves

## Fun Fact
You are using force right now! Gravity is pulling you down, and the chair is pushing you up (normal force) - that's why you don't fall through! 💪
