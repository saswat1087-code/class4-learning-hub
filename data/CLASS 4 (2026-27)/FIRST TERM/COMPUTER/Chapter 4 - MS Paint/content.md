# MS Paint - Drawing and Creativity

## Content
MS Paint is a drawing program that comes with Windows. It's a fun way to create pictures, draw shapes, add colors, and express your creativity! Even though it's simple, many artists started learning on MS Paint.

**Main Tools in MS Paint:**
- **Pencil Tool**: Draw freehand lines
- **Brush Tool**: Draw with different brush styles and sizes
- **Eraser Tool**: Remove parts of your drawing
- **Fill with Color**: Fill closed areas with color
- **Shape Tools**: Draw rectangles, circles, triangles, and more
- **Text Tool**: Add words to your picture
- **Color Picker**: Pick a color from your drawing
- **Magnifier**: Zoom in to see details

**File Menu Options:**
- New: Start a new drawing
- Open: Open an existing drawing
- Save: Save your drawing (use .png or .jpg)
- Print: Print your artwork

## Key Points
- MS Paint is a free drawing program in Windows
- Many tools: pencil, brush, shapes, text, fill color
- You can save and print your artwork
- Great for learning digital art basics
- Use Ctrl+Z to undo mistakes!

## Vocabulary
- **MS Paint**: Microsoft's drawing program
- **Canvas**: The white area where you draw
- **Toolbar**: Area with drawing tools
- **Color Palette**: Collection of colors you can use
- **Fill**: To color an entire closed area
- **Zoom**: To make the picture larger or smaller
- **Undo**: To reverse your last action

## Fun Fact
MS Paint has been included in every version of Windows since 1985! That's over 35 years! Many professional digital artists started with MS Paint. Some still use it to create amazing pixel art! 🎨
