# A Home Song

## Content

**Poem:**
Home is not just bricks and stone,
Not just a place to be alone.
A home is where the heart can rest,
Where love and kindness are the best.

It's where the family gathers near,
To share their joys, to wipe a tear.
The smell of food, the sound of song,
A place where everyone belongs.

The open window, dancing breeze,
The sunlight filtering through the trees.
A garden with its blooming flowers,
That grow through sun and rain-showers.

A home is built on love and care,
With happiness beyond compare.
So build your home with gentle hands,
The strongest home in all the lands.

**Summary:**
This beautiful poem describes how a true home is built on love, peace, and family bonds, rather than just bricks and mortar. A home is where hearts are happy, where family shares meals and stories, and where everyone feels safe and loved. The poet reminds us that home is not a building, but a feeling of belonging, warmth, and connection with family and nature.

## Key Points
- A home is made of love, not just bricks and walls
- Family makes a house into a home
- Nature (sunlight, breeze, flowers) is part of a happy home
- A home is where you feel safe, loved, and accepted
- The strongest homes are built with gentle hands and care

## Vocabulary
- **Home**: A place where you live with family and feel loved
- **Belonging**: Feeling accepted and wanted
- **Brick and mortar**: Building materials (symbolizing a physical house)
- **Hearth**: The heart of a home (fireplace)
- **Shelter**: A place that protects you
- **Comfort**: A feeling of ease and peace
- **Hospitality**: Welcoming guests warmly

## Fun Fact
The word "home" comes from the Old English word "ham" which meant "village" or "estate." It's over 1,000 years old! 🏠
