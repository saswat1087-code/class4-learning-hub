# What Robin Told Me

## Content
This is a charming nature poem where the poet listens to a robin bird singing. The robin shares secrets about the natural world and teaches valuable lessons about life.

The robin tells the poet about:
- The joy of waking up early in the morning
- The beauty of singing even when no one is listening
- The importance of building a safe home (nest)
- The happiness of raising a family
- The freedom of flying high in the sky

The robin teaches that true happiness comes from simple things - a warm nest, food for the family, the ability to sing freely, and being content with what you have.

## Key Points
- The robin bird shares wisdom with the poet
- True happiness comes from simple things
- Sing even if no one is listening (do your best always)
- Build a safe and loving home for your family
- Be content with what you have and enjoy freedom

## Vocabulary
- **Robin**: A small brown bird with a red breast
- **Nest**: A home built by birds
- **Content**: Happy and satisfied
- **Cherish**: To love and care for something
- **Freedom**: The power to act without restriction

## Fun Fact
Robins are known to sing at night as well as during the day, especially in places with bright street lights! 🐦
